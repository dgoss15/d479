Accommodations

Taniti offers a wide range of accommodations to suit every traveler's needs and budget. Choose from family-owned hotels, bed and breakfasts, or a luxurious resort.

Four-Star Resort

Experience luxury and relaxation with top-notch amenities and stunning ocean views.

Family-Owned Hotels

Enjoy a welcoming, local atmosphere in smaller, family-run establishments.

Bed and Breakfasts

Discover charming, intimate accommodations with personalized service.

Inexpensive Hostel

Perfect for budget travelers or backpackers looking to explore the island on a shoestring.

Beach Houses and Private Condos

Ideal for families or groups seeking private accommodations with direct access to the beach.
