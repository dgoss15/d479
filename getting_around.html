Getting Around

Traveling around Taniti is easy and convenient with multiple transportation options available to suit your needs.

Public Buses

Regular buses operate in Taniti City from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. daily, offering an affordable way to explore the city.

Private Buses

For destinations outside of Taniti City, private bus services are available to get you where you need to go.

Taxis

Easily accessible within Taniti City, taxis provide a quick and flexible way to get around.

Rental Cars

Rental cars are available near the airport, making it easy to explore the island at your own pace.

Bikes

Rent bikes and helmets from local vendors to enjoy the island's flat and scenic paths safely.

Walking

Many tourists find Taniti City and Merriton Landing to be highly walkable, making it easy to explore on foot.
