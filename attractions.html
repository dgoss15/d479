Attractions

Explore Taniti's stunning attractions, offering a blend of natural beauty and cultural experiences:

Beaches

Relax on the pristine sandy beaches encircling Yellow Leaf Bay, perfect for sunbathing and swimming.

Perfect Family Activity! Walking distance from most accomodations!

Rainforest

Embark on thrilling hikes or zip-line through the lush tropical rainforest.

These trips require either Private Buses, Taxi, or a Rental Car

Volcano

Witness the awe-inspiring active volcano, a must-see for adventure enthusiasts.

Only accessible by private tour buses, can be booked online!

Merriton Landing

Discover a bustling area with activities like snorkeling, visiting art galleries, enjoying the microbrewery, and more.

You can get to Merriton Landing by Bike, its the quickest and best way to see the sights!

Taniti City

Admire the native architecture and learn about the island's history through cultural tours.

City Buses will be your best way to explore the city.
