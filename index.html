About the Island

Taniti is a small, tropical island in the Pacific, known for its varied terrain, beautiful beaches, lush rainforests, and an active volcano. With an area of less than 500 square miles, Taniti offers an unforgettable travel experience for everyone.

Frequently Requested Information

  • Power outlets are 120 volts (the same as in the United States).
  • Alcohol is not allowed to be served or sold between midnight and 9:00 a.m.
  • The drinking age is 18 and not strictly enforced.
  • Many younger Tanitians speak fluent English, but little English is spoken in rural areas.
  • Taniti uses the U.S. dollar, and many businesses accept euros and yen.
  • Violent crime is very rare, but petty crimes like pickpocketing may occur.
  • National holidays may affect business hours and availability of attractions.
